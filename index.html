Rep One

Brand Overview

Meet Katherine K.

  • 27 years old
  • Comfortable with technology
  • Average level of fitness
  • Wants to look and feel stronger
  • Does some lifting on machines but struggles to hit goals or be consistent
  • Follows an informal diet based on info she found on internet
  • Has done free weights before, but always thinks she's doing it wrong
  • Has used a Fitbit and MyFitnessPal, without great results

She wants to get stronger. But sometimes she feels...

  • Frustrated when logging food and workouts in time-consuming apps
  • Out-of-place when working out in the same space as athletic gym-goers
  • Foolish when using equipment and exercises without training
  • Skeptical when reading workout plans and fitness advice that seems like it was written for other people
  • Trepidation about going to the gym for all of the reasons above
  • Guilty when she skips a day, even though she usually has an excuse

Katherine’s journey starts when she discovers Rep One

Habit building

1mo

It's not much yet, but Katherine can definitely see a difference. She can hardly believe it.

Experience

Exploration

6mo

Hits a plateau

Like all strength athletes, Katherine's progress has slowed considerably as she gets stronger. She asks her coach what to do.

7mo

Coach recommends new methodologies

Katherine tries circuit training and high intensity interval training (HIIT).

9mo

Tries new diet plan

Specialization

11mo

Focuses on HIIT full time

Katherine finds that she’s good at the training style and enjoys it.

13mo

Switches to a more advanced coach

Katherine finds a coach on the marketplace who specializes in HIIT

Mastery

15mo

Becoming her own coach

Katherine is capable of training herself and advancing her own fitness. She will return to coaching if she plateaus, but for now she decides to pick up exercise programs on the marketplace and follow them on her own.
